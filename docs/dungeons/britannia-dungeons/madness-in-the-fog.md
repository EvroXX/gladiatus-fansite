## Description

A long stone bridge, overgrown with moss, cuts through the thick fog hanging over the broad river. The voices of the long forgotten memories echo in your ears: whoseoever makes the crossing must confront not only their enemries, but their own sanity.

![Madness in the Fog](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Dungeons/Britannia/madness-in-the-fog/madness-in-the-fog.jpg "Madness in the Fog")

## Quests

1. Venture onto the eerie bridge and get across it.
   1. Cross the bridge
      1. **What are these creatures? They seem to appear from nowhere!**
   2. Fight the shadows
      1. **Don't give up**
   3. Reach the other side
      1. **Many warriors have already been pulled into the depths from the bridge. Watch out that you don't suffer the same fate**
2. Battle past the kelpie

## Glashtyn (Boss)

Glashtyn is a legendary creature from Manx folklore. The glashtin is said to be a goblin that appears out of its aquatic habitat, to come in contact with the island folk; others claim it takes the shape of a colt, or equate it to the water horse known locally as cabbyl-ushtey.
