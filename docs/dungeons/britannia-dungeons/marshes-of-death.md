## Description

A bleak moor lies before you, shrouded in mist, eerly quiet. A narrow walkway leads through the boggy marshes, whre strange figures briefly appear from the mist. Be careful! Not every leaves these marshes alive...

![Marshes of Death](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Dungeons/Britannia/marshes-of-death/marshes-of-death.jpg)

## Quests

1. Find a way through the creepy moor
   1. Cross the moor
      1. **There's only one way out of here, and it is before you. You have no other choice but to fight your way through!**
   2. Survive the attack
   3. Persevere
   4. Keep fighting
2. Defeat the giant snake
