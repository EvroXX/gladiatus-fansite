## Description

The Britons should have capitulated long since... yet their remaining warrios desperately battle on! Worse still, our own war elephants have turnd on us! What evil hand is behind all this?

![Storming the Coast](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Dungeons/Britannia/ashes-of-war/ashes_of_war.jpg "Storming the coast")

## Quests

1. Stop the crazed elephants
  1.1 **How could this happen? Something's not right here**
2. Find the source of the maddening magic
  2.1 **It seems to be some kind of a druidic magic emanating from the ruined cottage over there. Put and end to it!**
3. Put an end to the madness
