## Description

A winding forest path becomes a deathtrap when the enemy launches an attack from the undergrowth. The trees appear to swallow any movement, and the air is pregnant with danger. Only highly disciplined and watchful fighters can survive such an ambush.

![Forest Ambush](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Dungeons/Britannia/forest-ambush/forest-ambush.jpg "Forest Ambush")

## Quests

1. An ambush!
2. Survive the attack
3. Defeat the leader
