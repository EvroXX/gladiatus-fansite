## Description

Cassivellaunus' camp is surrounded by palisade walls and siege weapons. Your soldiers must defeat the enemry troops stationed without, before you can commence the siege proper. Only the strongest gladiators will be able to storm this final bastion.

![The Final Bastion](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Dungeons/Britannia/the-final-bastion/the-final-bation.jpg)

## Quests

1. Break through the palisade
    1. **The first defensive line has been broken! Don't give the remaining troops a chance to regroup!**
2. Push the defenders back
    1. **Finally, the enemy leader shows himself! Don't let him get away**
3. Defeat the camp leader
