# Optimal Build Simulation

Automatically find the optimal item combination for your character by setting the importance of each combat stat.
The simulator searches all valid items in your level range and runs a greedy multi-pass optimisation across all 9 equipment slots.

**How it works:**

1. Enter your character level and base training stats (or import them via your profile link).
2. Set stat priorities — how much you care about Critical Damage, Block, Damage, and Armour.
3. Choose the rarity at which to evaluate items.
4. Click **Find Optimal Build** and see the recommended gear with full stat breakdowns.

> The result is a greedy optimum — it may not be the global optimum for all possible combinations, but it converges quickly and gives strong practical results.
